Forgiveness in the Family!

At home is where we learn to truly love

Family is our School

God intended the family to be the first place where we learn:

  • To love and respect
  • To share and help
  • To ask for forgiveness and to forgive
“The family is the original cell of social life.” (CCC 2207)

What happens when we fight? ☁️

In every family there are:

  • Sibling fights
  • Words that hurt
  • Anger or misunderstandings

It's okay! We are all learning to love better.

“Forgive one another as the Lord has forgiven you.” (Colossians 3:13)

Forgiveness Heals the Heart! ❤️

When someone at home asks for forgiveness:

  • The heart calms down
  • The relationship is fixed
  • Love shines brighter
“The education of children takes place primarily in the family... learning to forgive.” (CCC 2227)

Jesus Unites the Family

Jesus gave His life to teach us that forgiveness is stronger than anger.

THE CROSS ✝️

It is the sign of God's total love.

“The cross of Christ is the sign of God’s total love for us.” (CCC 618)

ACTIVITY: Two Sheets and a Cross

1. On Sheet 1: Draw fights and anger.

2. On Sheet 2: Draw hugs and joy.

3. In the middle: Place the Cross of Jesus.

"Jesus unites our family with His love!"

CLOSING PRAYER

"Jesus, thank you for my family. Help us to forgive each other when we make mistakes. Fill our home with love, peace, and unity. Amen."
